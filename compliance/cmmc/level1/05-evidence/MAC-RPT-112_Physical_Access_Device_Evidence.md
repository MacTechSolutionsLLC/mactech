## 3. Physical Access Device Management

### 3.1 Device Inventory

**Inventory Process:**
- All physical access devices inventoried
- Device information recorded
- Inventory maintained and updated
- Inventory reviewed regularly

**Device Types:**
- Keys
- Access cards
- Badges
- Other physical access devices

---

### 3.2 Device Control

**Control Measures:**
- Device issuance controlled
- Device assignment tracked
- Device revocation process established
- Device accountability maintained
