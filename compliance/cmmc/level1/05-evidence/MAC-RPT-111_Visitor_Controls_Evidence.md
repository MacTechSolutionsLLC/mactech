## 3. Visitor Escort and Monitoring Procedures

### 3.1 Visitor Procedures

**Escort Requirements:**
- Visitors are escorted by authorized personnel
- Visitors are supervised during entire visit
- Visitors are not left unattended
- Visitors do not access system or view CUI

**Monitoring Requirements:**
- Visitor activity monitored during visit
- Visitor access restricted to authorized areas
- Visitor access logged (if applicable)
- Visitor procedures documented

---

### 3.2 Visitor Log Template

**Visitor Log Information:**
- Visitor name
- Visitor organization
- Visit date and time
- Purpose of visit
- Escort name
- Areas accessed
- Visitor signature
- Escort signature

**Log Maintenance:**
- Visitor logs maintained
- Logs reviewed regularly
- Logs retained per retention policy
