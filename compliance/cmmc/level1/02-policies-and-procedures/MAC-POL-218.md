## 1. Policy Statement

MacTech Solutions maintains audit and accountability controls to ensure the security and integrity of organizational systems and information. This policy establishes requirements for audit and accountability in accordance with CMMC Level 2 requirements and NIST SP 800-171 Rev. 2, Section 3.3.

---

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to:
- All organizational systems
- All personnel with system access
- All audit and accountability activities
- All Federal Contract Information (FCI) and Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI)

**System Scope:** FCI and CUI environment.
