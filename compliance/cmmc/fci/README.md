## FCI Definition

**FCI (Federal Contract Information):** Information, not intended for public release, that is provided by or generated for the Government under a contract to develop or deliver a product or service to the Government, but not including information that is lawfully publicly available without restrictions.

**MacTech Solutions FCI Scope:**
- Only non-public information related to government contracts is treated as FCI
- Publicly released information (e.g., SAM.gov postings) is not FCI unless combined with internal, non-public data
