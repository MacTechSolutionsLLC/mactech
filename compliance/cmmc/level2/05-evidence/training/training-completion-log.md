## 1. Purpose

This document tracks security awareness training completion for all personnel with access to the MacTech Solutions system.

---

## 2. Training Requirements

**Initial Training:**
- Required before system access is granted
- Covers general security awareness
- Covers role-specific responsibilities
- Completion acknowledgment required

**Annual Training:**
- Required annually for all personnel
- Updates on new policies and procedures
- Refresher on security requirements
- Completion tracked and documented

**Role-Specific Training:**
- Required when roles change
- Covers role-specific security responsibilities
- Completion required for new role
